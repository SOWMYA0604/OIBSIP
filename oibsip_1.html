Environmental Issues

Soil Pollution

                          Soil contamination, soil pollution
                         as a part of land degradation is caused by
                        the presence of xenobiotic (human-made) chemicals.

Water Pollution

                        Water pollution (or aquatic pollution) is the contamination
                         water bodies, usually as a result of human activities, 
                         so that it negatively affects its uses

Air Pollution

                        Air pollution is the contamination of air 
                        due to the presence of substances in the 
                        atmosphere that are harmful to the health 
                        of humans and other living beings, or
                         cause damage to the climate or to 
                         materials.
